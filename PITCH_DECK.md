## Slide 8: Business Model

### Revenue Streams

**1. Freemium Model**
- Free basic features (swipes, matches, messaging)
- Premium subscriptions for enhanced features

**2. Premium Features (Tiered Pricing)**
- **Basic ($9.99/month):** Unlimited swipes, see who likes you
- **Premium ($19.99/month):** All basic + undo swipes, boost profile, read receipts
- **VIP ($29.99/month):** All premium + priority matching, verified badge

- Advertising (sponsored profiles)
- Partnership deals (event sponsorships)
