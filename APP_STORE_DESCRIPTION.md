## App Description (up to 4000 characters)

Move beyond surface-level swiping. Known is a dating app designed for people who value depth, authenticity, and meaningful connections.

**Find Someone Who Truly Gets You**

Known uses a thoughtful compatibility system that goes far beyond profile pictures. Through engaging, conversational questions, we help you discover people who align with your values, communication style, and vision for the future.

**How It Works**

1. **Answer Thoughtful Questions**: Instead of filling out endless forms, engage in meaningful conversations about what matters most—life direction, emotional readiness, communication style, lifestyle alignment, and commitment vision.

2. **Deep Compatibility Matching**: Our algorithm connects you with people who share your core values and relationship goals, not just surface-level interests.

3. **Authentic Connections**: Start conversations with people who already understand what you're looking for, making every match more meaningful.

**Why Known is Different**

- **Conversation-Based Profiles**: No boring forms or checkboxes. Answer questions through an intimate chat interface that feels natural and engaging.

- **Compatibility-First**: We match you based on what truly matters for long-term relationships—values, communication, and life goals.

- **Quality Over Quantity**: Focus on meaningful connections rather than endless swiping through profiles.

- **Privacy-Focused**: Your information is secure, and you control who sees your profile.

**Perfect For**

- People seeking serious, meaningful relationships
- Those tired of superficial dating apps
- Individuals who value depth and authenticity
- Anyone looking for someone who truly understands them

**Start Your Journey**

Download Known today and discover what it means to connect with someone on a deeper level. Answer thoughtful questions, explore compatibility, and find your person—beyond the profile picture.

Where deep connections begin.
